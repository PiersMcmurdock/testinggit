Creating HTML5 Video with Flash fallback
Create video files in formats which are appropriate for your audience. These may include .mp4 , WebM , .WMV etc you will need either .swf or .flv for your flash fallback. The following code below can be used as a template for creating HTML5 video with flash fallback.

<video>
  <source src="file name.mp4" type="video/mp4">
  <source src="file name.ogg" type="video/ogg">
  <embed src="swf or flv video.flv"
  type="application/x-shockwave-flash"
  width="1024" height="798"
  allowscriptaccess="always" allowfullscreen="true"></embed>
</video>
